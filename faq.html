PensionKeyGlobal does not give any form of investment or tax advice. We act as a provider of information only for UK pension fund holders wishing to reduce debt or raise funds in the form of a loan attached to a SSAS. A SSAS is deemed an occupational pension scheme which is not, and does not need to be, regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority. The intention of this website and the content contained within is to inform UK pension fund holders who find it difficult to raise mainstream funds that a SSAS loan is a legal, tax efficient and HMRC approved alternative.

PensionKeyGlobal can put you in touch with many experts and long established SSAS providers so that you can make an informed decision as to if a SSAS loan is right for you and your circumstances.
