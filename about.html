Laid Back Loans does not give any form of investment or tax advice. A SSAS is deemed an occupational pension scheme which is not, and does not need to be, regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority. The intention of this website and the content contained within is to inform UK pension fund holders who find it difficult to raise mainstream funds that a SSAS loan is a legal, tax efficient and HMRC approved alternative.
